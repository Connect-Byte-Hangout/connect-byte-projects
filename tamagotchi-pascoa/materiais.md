## Materials

### Basic Project:
• LED

• Resistor (220Ω)

• Push-button

• Coin Battery (CR2032)

• Jumper wires

### Intermediate Project
• LED

• 220Ω or 150Ω resistors

• Buzzer

• Sound sensor (KY-038)

• Arduino (Nano or Uno)

• Jumper wires

• USB cable

• Breadboard (optional)

• Battery holder and AA batteries (optional)
